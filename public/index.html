Overview

A simple timeline for getting started

You don't have to plan everything at once. EventFlow is designed so you can make progress in small, confident steps — whether you're organising a wedding, party or work function.

1. Set your direction

Decide roughly what you're planning, when it'll happen and how many people you're inviting.

2. Shortlist venues & key suppliers

Browse suppliers, open a few that feel right and add them to your plan so everything stays together.

3. Start a few conversations

Message the suppliers that stand out. Ask about availability, pricing and what they recommend.

4. Lock things in

Once you're happy, confirm your favourites directly with each supplier and update your plan.
